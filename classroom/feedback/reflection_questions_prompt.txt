# Title: Create Reflection Questions for Math Learning
# Category: Classroom / Feedback
# Goal: To promote metacognitive reflection after lessons.
# Author: Anshid
# Last Updated: 2025-10-18

## Prompt:
Create 5 reflection questions that help students think about what they learned in {topic_name}.  
Use prompts like:
- “What surprised you about…?”
- “Where might you use this idea?”
- “What mistake taught you the most?”
Output in bullet list format.
