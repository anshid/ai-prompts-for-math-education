# Title: Generate Personalized Student Feedback
# Category: Classroom / Feedback
# Goal: To produce constructive, encouraging feedback for math students.
# Author: Anshid
# Last Updated: 2025-10-18

## Prompt:
Given a student's answer and the correct solution,  
generate short, personalized feedback focusing on:
- What they did well
- What concept needs clarification
- A gentle next-step suggestion

Keep tone encouraging and growth-oriented.
