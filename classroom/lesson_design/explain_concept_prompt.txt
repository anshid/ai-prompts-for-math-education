# Title: Explain a Mathematical Concept Step-by-Step
# Category: Classroom / Lesson Design
# Goal: To generate clear, layered explanations of mathematical concepts.
# Usage: Replace {concept_name} with your topic.
# Author: Anshid
# Last Updated: 2025-10-18

## Prompt:
You are a math educator helping students understand {concept_name}.  
Explain it in four layers:
1. Intuitive analogy
2. Conceptual explanation
3. Worked example
4. Common misconception and how to fix it
Format your response in Markdown.
